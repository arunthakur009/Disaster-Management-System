  1. Stay calm and assess your immediate safety
  2. Move to higher ground during floods
  3. For fires, evacuate perpendicular to wind direction
  4. After earthquakes, beware of aftershocks and damaged structures
  5. If trapped, conserve phone battery and use SOS signals
